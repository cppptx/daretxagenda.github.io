This isn't your ordinary conference, y'all.

Happening November 30 - December 1 in Austin at The Hyatt Regency, the Dare Texas Summit dares our state to be the best for hard-working people and their families. From art to storytelling—and yes, policy analysis—we will explore how to make Texas the best state for everyone—across income, ZIP code, gender, race and ethnicity.


On the heels of contentious national elections and just ahead of the 2017 Texas Legislative session, the DARE Texas Summit will take place at an important moment for our state's future. Attendees will get the policy background and connections they need to help shape the state they want for future generations.


A talented and influential lineup of plenary speakers.
